# Lunaria Atelier design direction

source: image reference and Instagram profile supplied by the user

## Character

Lunaria Atelier is a luminous editorial commerce demo for handmade natural stone jewelry. It combines generous serif headlines, tactile product photography, pearly silk backgrounds and layered commerce cards. The result feels feminine, natural and personal. It is premium without becoming formal or inaccessible.

## Build mandate

Every public screen should place the jewelry and the customer story first. Use warm ivory surfaces, sand, soft cocoa and restrained gold. A light champagne silk texture is the unifying background material. Keep the demonstration label visible but discreet. Avoid dark luxury styling, generic marketplace layouts and technical language.

## Composition patterns

Use wide editorial sections, asymmetrical image and copy pairs, soft overlapping cards and occasional rounded dark cocoa panels for contrast. One continuous champagne silk background runs behind the whole page. Sections stay transparent so the fabric never restarts and no horizontal seams appear. Clean paper surfaces are reserved for contained editorial cards. Let product cards overlap the preceding image area on large screens. On mobile, return all layers to a clear single column.

## Component and usage rules

Cards use generous rounding, warm borders and diffuse shadows. Buttons are pill shaped, compact and clearly prioritised. Hover feedback varies by role: primary actions shimmer and lift, secondary actions softly fill, product arrows rotate, chips invert and navigation links draw a gold underline. Product photography uses large crops with descriptive alt text. Configuration choices behave like tactile sample cards. Scroll motion stays soft and respects reduced motion preferences.

## Responsive behavior

Stack editorial layouts below the large breakpoint. Product grids become horizontally scrollable only where they support discovery, otherwise they stack. All controls remain at least 44 pixels tall. The configurator keeps its price and next action visible near the bottom on mobile.

## Tokens

Token values live in `app/globals.css`. Tailwind utility classes remain the implementation source of truth.
